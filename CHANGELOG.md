# Changelog

All notable changes to VAG CODER will be documented here.

## [1.0.0] - 2026-03-30

### Initial Release

- **76 community-verified tweaks** across 20 ECU modules for 2019 Audi RS5 (B9) US-Spec
- **Web Bluetooth** connection to OBDeleven and ELM327 BLE dongles
- **Full ECU backup & restore** — reads coding from 28 module addresses, saves to JSON
- **Compatibility badges** — each tweak tagged as YOUR CAR ✓, option-dependent, or NOT COMPATIBLE
- **Raw UDS terminal** with quick-command buttons
- **Queue system** for batching multiple changes
- **Search** across all tweaks from the Modules tab
- **Security access PINs** displayed per module (09: 20113, 46: 20103, 03: 40168, etc.)
- **Safety warnings** including hood-open requirement for 2019+ models

### Modules Included

09 Central Electronics, 17 Instrument Cluster, 5F MMI, 46 Comfort, 08 HVAC, 02 Transmission, 01 Engine ECU, 19 CAN Gateway, 03 ABS, 13 ACC, A5 Front Sensors, 44 Steering Assist, 36 Driver Seat, A9 Soundaktor, CA Sunroof, 16 Steering Column, 82 HUD, 42/52 Door Electronics, 06 Passenger Seat, 75 Telematics
